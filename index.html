REBOUND Conference 2024

A fully remote two day meeting on the N-body integrator REBOUND: new developments, hands-on demos, science results. July 8th and 9th 2024. The abstract submission deadline has now past but you can still sign up to attend.

What to expect.

REBOUND24

A small meeting that brings together both people who primarily develop and people who primarily use REBOUND.

Technical Talks

Talks on the technical details of REBOUND including numerical integrators.

Science results

Talks about the science results enabled by REBOUND.

Fully remote

Fully remote on ZOOM including several social events.

Date and time

Conference dates are July 8th and 9th 2024, with talks taking place from 9am to 2pm EDT both days to allow for participation from both America and Europe. The times might change depending on the location of the majority of participants.

No fee

No registration fee.

Registrations

As of May 19th 2024, we have almost 200 registered participants from all around the world.
